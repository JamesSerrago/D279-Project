Understanding the Relational Needs of Your Bird

Birds are intelligent, social creatures that require emotional care and interaction to thrive. Learn how to build a strong bond and support your bird’s well-being.

Why Emotional Health Matters

Birds come in all sizes—from the tiny Cuban bee hummingbird to the massive ostrich. While many birds fly, some do not. Despite their differences, all birds share a need for social interaction and mental stimulation.

Signs of Depression in Birds

  • Reduced appetite
  • Increased irritability
  • Aggressive behavior
  • Changes in song tone (more solemn or quiet)

If you notice these signs, consult your veterinarian. Early detection can significantly improve your bird’s health and longevity.
